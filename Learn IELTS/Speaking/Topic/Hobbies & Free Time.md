#### **2. Sentence Structures (How to Build Your Answer)**

A good structure is to give a general answer and then explain the reason.

*   **Structure A:** "**It really depends on my workload.** On a typical week, I try to **set aside**..., but when I'm **swamped with work**, I barely have any time at all."
*   **Structure B:** "I try to be consistent and **dedicate** about... hours each weekend. I find that having a routine helps me to..."
*   **Structure C:** "**To be honest, not as much as I'd like.** I usually only manage to squeeze in **a couple of hours here and there**. I really wish I had more time to **devote to** it."

#### **3. Full Answer Example (Band 7.0)**

> "**Honestly, it varies quite a bit from week to week.** On a **typical week**, I try to **set aside** maybe three to four hours, mostly on the weekend. However, when I'm **swamped with work** and facing a **tight deadline**, my hobby is unfortunately the first thing I have to sacrifice."

**Analysis of the example:**
*   It gives a realistic, "it depends" answer which is very natural.
*   It uses excellent, idiomatic vocabulary ("varies quite a bit," "typical week," "set aside," "swamped with work," "tight deadline").
*   It clearly explains the reason for the variation in time (workload), showing an ability to explain cause and effect.

**"Are there any differences between the hobbies of young people and old people?"**

This is a classic comparison question. It tests your ability to talk about general groups of people and use comparative language (e.g., "whereas," "in contrast," "on the other hand"). A strong answer will highlight a key difference and provide examples.

#### **2. Sentence Structures (How to Build Your Answer)**

A clear structure is to state the difference and then give an example for each group.

*   **Structure A:** "Yes, I think there are **significant differences**. The **younger generation tends to** enjoy..., **whereas the older generation often prefers** activities like..."
*   **Structure B:** "**Absolutely.** Their interests are often **worlds apart**, mainly due to different **lifestyles**. Young people are generally drawn to..., **in contrast,** older adults..."
*   **Structure C:** "Generally speaking, yes. While it's not true for everyone, you often find that young people prefer more **physically demanding** hobbies, **on the other hand,** a common pastime for seniors is..."

#### **3. Full Answer Example (Band 7.0)**

> "**Yes, I believe there are some quite significant differences**, which I think are mainly related to energy levels and technology. The **younger generation** is often drawn to more **tech-savvy** or **physically demanding** hobbies like online gaming or team sports. **In contrast**, the **older generation** might prefer more **sedentary** and relaxing **pastimes**, such as gardening or reading, which require less physical exertion."

**Analysis of the example:**
*   It gives a clear and direct answer.
*   It identifies two key reasons for the difference (energy and technology).
*   It uses excellent comparative vocabulary ("significant differences," "in contrast").
*   It uses specific, high-quality descriptive words ("tech-savvy," "physically demanding," "sedentary," "pastimes").
*   It provides clear examples for both age groups.
